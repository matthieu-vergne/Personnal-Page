SOLID Principles

SOLID principles are rather well known in software engineering. You can look at Wikipedia for overall explanations. Although we provide a summary of what they are in each article, we focus here on how they support generic programming in Java.

  • Single responsibility
  • Open/closed
  • Liskov substitution
  • Interface segregation
